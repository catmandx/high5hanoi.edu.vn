High5 Hanoi is a non-profit organization. The club was founded by Minh Nguyen and Diep Nguyen on May 24th, 2014. High5 Hanoi aims at helping foreigners integrate into the working and living culture in Hanoi, understanding the beauty of Vietnamese culture and traditions.

The Story of High5 Hanoi

From the very basic

Vietnamese lessons at the beginner level help you communicate effectively with the locals, or at least survive.

Best-fit lessons

01 induction meeting, 13 lessons, 02 tests, and 01 final meetings. Realistic outcomes, expectations and real fun.

Live local, speak local

This is not an intensive course so do not depend only on High5 Hanoi. Go outside, do homework and put things in practice!

Flexible Schedule

One-on-one lessons with High5 members give you the most freedom to create a timetable that works best.

Cultural Events

Vietnamese culture-themed events help you know, experience, and learn about traditions, handicrafts, costumes, etc.

Hang-out

Hang out, chitchat, discuss, find your local friends, have quality time, and feel connected.
